Social Media — Menu

A menu of content formats, not a fixed calendar.

Pick a mix based on what's sustainable to shoot and edit — this is the plan, execution stays with Griggs or a future office hire. Each format is tagged with the brand pillar it proves and which ICP tier it speaks to most directly.

Pillar: Flawless Execution

Proof that the work is genuinely master-level, without saying so directly.

POV job-site clips (smart glasses)

Low effort to shoot

Shot hands-free on Meta Ray-Ban glasses while working — since an electrician's hands are busy, this is the only realistic way to capture real job footage. Voiceover gets added afterward in editing, explaining what's happening and why it matters.

A Note on Cadence

None of this needs to run daily to work. A steady drip of 2–3 posts a week, mixing low-effort formats (reviews, before/afters) with the occasional higher-effort one (detail-of-the-day, community posts), is enough to keep the feed alive without turning content into a second job.
