Positioning

A master electrician's business that happens to feel like family.

This is the sentence everything else on this page has to hold up.

"Griggs Electric charges like professionals because it works like one — but every job still feels like a neighbor showing up to help, not a contractor billing hours."

The Balance

Friendly enough to trust in your home. Disciplined enough to trust behind your walls.

Neither side of this gets to win. Too much "family" and the pricing feels unjustified. Too much "master craftsman" and the warmth disappears. Every piece of marketing needs both at once.

Family Side

Warm, personal, plainspoken. Shows up like a relative, not a vendor. Treats the customer's home like their own.

balanced against
Master Side

Licensed Master Electrician-led. Code-perfect, detail-obsessed, zero-callback execution. Priced like the premium work it is.

Premium Pricing Philosophy

Not the cheapest bid — the one you don't have to think about again.

The price reflects what the customer is actually buying, and marketing needs to make that case clearly, without ever sounding defensive about it.
